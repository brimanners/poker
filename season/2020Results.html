Season wrap up

2020 - Well, what can we say. Despite the carnage that Covid-19 brought to the poker calender, we did manage to get two successful nights in out of 6 proposed dates.

13 lucky people turned up at Brian's for the years kick off event and Michael took down the honours and also winning 2 out of his last 3 games. Impressive run of form. He pipped Ben to the goodies with Dave improving from his usual fourth to fifth place.

The beers were definitely flowing on this one and everyone was on form leaving plenty of memories and visuals to forget.

And the Corono hit, and the much anticipated game at Jades was cancelled.

Then with a lull in covid cases, but then a resurgence starting to happen, and Dan the man/Dictator Dan *delete as applicable let us have another event, with Mark hosting 11 players, just 24 hours before indoor limits were going to be reduced to 5 people.

Ben was the event winner, pipping Carlos in second and Brian in 3rd. Another amazing pre-game feed from the MasterChef Mark and a good night was had by all

And that was it for 2021. A 3 month lockdown to endure and then with the covid tail wagging before donut days were upon us, it was too late to host any more games.

So Ben is officially the 2020 champion. It's fair to say that this status will no doubt not have the same weight as previous seasons, but stats are stats. Congats to Ben!!

And subdued congrats to Dave taking the silver and Michael into the medal tables with bronze. Maan has the unlucky tag of receiving the wooder spoon, but it doesn't really feel fair after only 2 games :)

Who knows what 2021 will bring - Maybe we'll have to show a vaccination certificate before play, but we will have to wait and see!
