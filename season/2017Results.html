Season wrap up

The 2017 poker fifth season had 6 events for the August Square poker fraternity to frequent, with Brian the reigning champion hoping for another good season.

Brian kicked off the season with 7 people enjoying some summertime pizza, and for once home advantage did not come into play. In fact Brian came last and it was Ben that came flying out of the blocks to take the honours with Junior in the runner up spot.

2016 Champion Brian handing over the trophy to the 2017 champion Brian :)

Mark V then hosted the next event with 9 players fighting it out on a balmy Autumn evening. Making amends for his last place finish, Brian turned the tables around and grabbed the main prize. Andy also gathered some well earned points on the ladder with his second place. Ben still held onto the lead on the ladder with a 3 point gap to Brian.

Simon offered up his abode and his customary paella dish for the third event and it was Steve's time to shine as he won a close and hard fought victory against Carlos for the top spots. With Ben unable to make the event due to illness, there was 3 way tie at the top of the ladder with Brian, Steve and Ben all on 21 points, but Ben still in 1st as he had played less games.

Ben was next to host with 7 players fighting it out with guest commentary by Mark L. Ben again took the honours with Mark L in second place. Ben topped the ladder with a 4 point buffer ahead of Brian.

The penultimate game had Steve hosting a new square record of 14 players, so lots of points were on offer. A two table event saw three new players making their debuts, Andrew L, Kevin and Jacob. A long night of cards eventuated with Brian taking all the points, with Jade in second and Ben in third. The top two ladder positions swapped places with Brian now 2 points ahead of Ben with one game to go.

With the final game taking place at Mark L's, nine players enjoyed some pre-festive spirit with some great pre-game food preparations. The game itself played out with Ben going out in fourth, which meant Brian would take out the season and did so in style, winning the last game, with Dave in second and Steve in third.

Despite getting his first last place in 5 years on the first event, Brian managed to claw enough points back with the remaining games, and win 3 out of the 6 by season end to take out the gold medal and 66 points.

Ben accumulated 55 points after good placings in all games and winning 2 games out of 5 to take out the silver medal Who knows what the table may have looked like if he wasn't sick for one of the events...but you gotta be in it, to win it.

Steve played a straight bat throughout the season with one win and ended up with a very respectable bronze medal to add to his medal collection.

Dave ended up just missing out the medals with his customary 4th place finishing on 31 points, and at the time of writing, Dave is 4th on the all seasons ladder and also 4th on the recent form ladder. Go figure :)

Wishing everyone a wonderful festive season with family and friends. Raising a glass to hopefully a wonderful 2018 and another great year of poker.
