Recommended blind levels

 

....keep an eye out or be blinded out!

With a starting stack of $5,000, we'll adhere to the following blind levels and durations which should make one tournament last approximately 3 to 4 hours.

Losing all your chips prior to the first break, you will automatically receive $5,000, with the balance of $10,000 at the first break (or earlier if you lose the secondary $5,000). If your stack goes below $5,000 and you've already received your free chip-up, you are eligible for a re-buy up to the 3rd break.

Chip denominations: $25 (green), $100 (black), $500 (purple), $1,000 (yellow), $5,000 (orange)

Starting stack: 12 x $25 ($300) + 12 x $100 ($1,200) + 3 x $500 ($1,500) + 2 x $1,000 ($2,000) = $5,000
