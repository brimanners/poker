Recommended blind levels

 

....keep an eye out or be blinded out!

With a starting stack of $1000, we'll adhere to the following blind levels and durations which should make one tournament last approximately 3 to 4 hours.

Note: If your stack goes below $500 and you've already received your free chip-ups, you are eligible for a re-buy up to the 3rd break.

Level Duration Small Blind Big Blind Ante Notes
1st 30 mins $5 $10
2nd 30 mins $10 $20
Break 1 10 mins Extra $1000 worth of chips. Colouring up and removing $5 and $10 chips
3rd 30 mins $25 $50
4th 30 mins $50 $100
Break 2 10 mins Extra $2000 worth of chips. Colouring up and removing $25 chips
5th 20 mins $100 $200
6th 20 mins $200 $400 $50
7th 20 mins $300 $600 $50
Break 3 10 mins Last chance for a re-buy of $2000. Colouring up and removing $50 chips.
8th 20 mins $500 $1,000 $100
9th 20 mins $1,000 $2,000 $200
10th 20 mins $2,000 $4,000 $500
11th Unlimited $5,000 $10,000 $1000
